Dr. Lodewijk Brand

Hi! My name is Lodewijk "Lou" Brand and I have spent the past four years working in the MInDS@Mines Lab directed by Dr. Hua Wang. My research is centered on the development of scalable machine learning algorithms applied to problems in bioinformatics with a focus on problems involving multi-modal and multi-instance data. I have applied my research to identify which genetic variants and regions of the brain contribute to a future diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease. When I'm not in the lab, I spend the majority of my time rock-climbing outside.

Dr. Daniel Pittman

Hello there! I'm Professor Daniel Pittman, an Associate Professor of the Practice in Software Development and Architecture in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Denver (DU). I worked in the software industry for 13 years, most recently as a principal software architect at Comcast, and have just recently started a full-time position at DU. Since joining DU, I have taught Web Programming I, Web Programming II, Web Programming III (Web Projects), NoSQL Databases, Computer Forensics, and Machine Learning.
