About Our Camps

OUR MISSION

Founded by two Cherry Creek High School Computer Science students, our camps aim to foster interest in computer science for middle school students.

OUR PROCESS

We teach the basics of computer science using the coding languages Processing and Java. We achieve this with a mixture of engaging lessons, notes, games, labs, and worksheets. Click on Sessions and Information to learn more!

OUR IMPACT

Past students go on to pave the way for computer science in their communities. Many are taking or signed up to take computer science courses at Cherry Creek High School. Others have founded their own programming clubs! Some have contacted us with questions about coding or for extra resources.
